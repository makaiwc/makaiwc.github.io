Contribution to Expanded Field, May 2015
Canvas, rice paper, pine, and aluminum; 4' x 4' x 4'
Expanded Field was a temporary campus-wide installation of experiential, rectilinear forms. Approximately twenty students built eleven structures that could be entered; we coordinated to ensure a variety of materials and shapes. Neither sculpture nor architecture in the strictest senses, our boxes played with comfort, discomfort, and the ability of a space to dictate experience. March 2016
Oil on canvas; 3'x 4'
